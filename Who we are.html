How We Started

Carolina Cold Ones burst onto the scene when Matthew Moyer, a fraternity brother with a keen eye for fashion (or at least for less atrocious fraternity shirts), decided he had endured enough of the overpriced, underwhelming, and slow-as-molasses t-shirt services. With the enthusiasm of a freshman at their first tailgate, Matthew began producing shirts for his fraternity brothers, quickly extending his reach to the wardrobes of his friends and family as well. He wasn’t merely splashing paint on fabric; he was rescuing his brothers from the fashion blunders of dull, overpriced tees.

Where We Are Today

From humble beginnings serving Matthew's fraternity brothers, we've grown and evolved into a company that caters to businesses and organizations across various sectors. But we didn't stop there. Today, we're thrilled to announce the launch of our own exclusive clothing line—a collection designed not just to impress but to make a difference. Meet your new favorite wardrobe staples that do more than just look good. Our clothing line is committed to giving back, donating a percentage of its profits to animal charities around the world. Every purchase you make helps support vital causes that protect and nurture animals in need.
